APP FEATURES

Localify is your one-stop shop for discovering the amazing businesses that fuel your community. Whether you're a resident seeking the perfect local spot or a business owner looking to connect with new customers, Localify empowers you to do the following;

Explore with Ease

Navigate a comprehensive database of local businesses across various categories. Find everything from restaurants and shops to service providers and entertainment venues - all within your city or neighborhood.

Make Informed Decisions

Explore more thoroughly with our comprehensive listing information. Gain in-depth knowledge about the business. Access a wealth of contact information, such as phone numbers, and physical locations, all conveniently compiled in one place. Whether you're looking to get in touch with customer service, inquire about services, or visit in person, everything you need is easily accessible. Our goal is to ensure that you have all the necessary information at your fingertips to make informed decisions and connect seamlessly with the business.

Effortless Bulk Upload

Manage your business listings efficiently. Upload a CSV file containing your business data for streamlined registration and updates (perfect for franchises or businesses with multiple locations).

About Us

Localify was founded with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between local businesses and their communities. We envision a world where local businesses thrive, and residents can effortlessly discover the hidden gems and trusted services right in their own neighborhoods. By fostering connections and empowering informed choices, Localify aims to revitalize local economies and cultivate vibrant communities where everyone benefits.


Localify is driven by a passionate team of professionals who are dedicated to innovation and user experience. We come from diverse backgrounds, sharing a common love for supporting local businesses and fostering a strong sense of community. Our team is constantly working behind the scenes to improve the platform, expand our reach, and create a valuable resource for both business owners and local residents.


Localify is more than just a platform - it's a movement. We believe in the power of local businesses to shape the character and soul of our communities. By supporting local, we invest in the future of our neighborhoods and contribute to a more sustainable and vibrant society. Whether you're a business owner looking to connect with local customers or a resident seeking to discover the best your city has to offer, we invite you to join the Localify movement and become a part of something bigger.


Christian Chi is the Sole Developer of this application and you can catch him up on Twitter, Linedin or Github. The codebase for localify is available on Github via this repo.
